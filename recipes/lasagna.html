Lasagna

This is the recipe for the "World's Best Lasagna." Image and recipe provided by AllRecipes.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. - AllRecipes

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step to Step Guide:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe information.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
